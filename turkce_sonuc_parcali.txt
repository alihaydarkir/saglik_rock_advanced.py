=== KISA SORU ===

As an emergency medicine specialist, answer concisely:

For adult ROSC after cardiac arrest:
1. First medications used?
2. Dose calculation methods? 
3. Infusion vs push?
4. Intubation effects?

Short professional response:


=== İNGİLİZCE YANIT ===
**1. First medications used?**
- Cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) should be initiated immediately.
- Defibrillation should be started if indicated.

**2. Dose calculation methods?**
- The American Heart Association (AHA) guidelines recommend a target dose of 300-400 mg of epinephrine intravenously over 5-10 minutes.

**3. Infusion vs push?**
- Intravenous (IV) infusion.
- Push is not recommended for ROSC.

**4. Intubation effects?**
- Intubation can be necessary for mechanical ventilation and protection from aspiration.
- It is not necessary for ROSC.
